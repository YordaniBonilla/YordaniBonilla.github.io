About me

I was born in Tepic, Nayarit, lived in Marquezado a small ranch. When I was 6 my dad immigrated to the U.S.A., 2 years later we moved to California for a while then moved to Washington State where we would settle down for a while. There I went to JJ smith elementary after that Enumclaw Middle and finally Enumclaw High School. After leaving school I got experience at my first job washing cars it was neat, easy job good money and I also got to drive a mustang!. Then I decided to leave the U.S. and return to Mexico, my life wasn't easy, don't think anyones is. So I returned to Mexico and lived in H. Jara with my grandma. The work was hard. They pay was bad. But it was a change from the life in the us. Here I had all the freedom I could go anywhere without worrying about getting in trouble and getting deported. I moved to Tijuana city for a while to work in call centers because I heard the pay was good. I didn't like it one bit. Later I just moved back to the ranch now living in H. Jara.
